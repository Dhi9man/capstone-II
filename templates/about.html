How It Works

Detection Pipeline

A multi-stage forensic analysis pipeline combining classical signal processing with deep learning.

01

Audio Feature Extraction

Using librosa, we extract 40-dimensional MFCC (Mel-Frequency Cepstral Coefficients), chroma features, spectral centroid, and RMS energy at 16kHz. These capture the phonetic content of speech with high temporal resolution.

librosa.feature.mfcc(y, sr=16000, n_mfcc=40)
02

Lip Motion Detection

Using OpenCV Haar cascade classifiers, we locate the face and approximate the lip region (lower 35% of detected face bounding box). Lip pixel intensity variance tracks mouth openness over time.

cv2.CascadeClassifier('haarcascade_frontalface')
03

Cross-Correlation Sync Analysis

The core detection signal: we compute the normalized cross-correlation between the audio RMS energy envelope and the lip motion signal. Real videos show high zero-lag correlation. Deepfakes show phase-shifted or decorrelated signals due to re-synthesized audio or lip animation.

scipy.signal.correlate(lip_motion, audio_rms)
04

Artifact Analysis

Deepfake models introduce subtle blending artifacts at face boundaries. We use Laplacian sharpness variance comparison between the face region and background — discrepancies indicate GAN-based face synthesis.

cv2.Laplacian(face_roi, cv2.CV_64F).var()
05

Temporal Consistency

Frame-to-frame delta analysis measures video smoothness. Deepfake models often produce temporal jitter or inconsistent frame transitions, captured by the coefficient of variation of inter-frame differences.

delta.std() / delta.mean() # CV score
06

Sync Transformer Model

A lightweight PyTorch Transformer (2 encoder layers, 4 attention heads) ingests joint audio-visual embeddings and outputs a binary authenticity score. Architecture inspired by SyncNet (Chung & Zisserman, 2016).

nn.TransformerEncoder(layer, num_layers=2)
07

Weighted Ensemble

Final score is a weighted combination: Sync (40%) + Artifacts (25%) + Temporal (20%) + Model (15%). Threshold at 0.5 determines the DEEPFAKE / AUTHENTIC verdict with confidence percentage.

score = 0.40·sync + 0.25·artifact + 0.20·temporal + 0.15·model
